## The Five Dimensions

### 1. Evidence Axis

Question: What proof exists?

States:

- NONE
- CLAIMED
- CAPTURED
- HASHED
- RECEIPTED
- VERIFIED

Rule: no evidence axis promotion without a receipt.

### 2. Authority Axis

Question: Who is allowed to say or do this?

States:

- PERSONAL
- OPERATOR
- CANDIDATE
- REVIEWED
- PROMOTED
- OFFICIAL_EXTERNAL

Rule: external authority requires public evidence. Agency names do not create authority.

### 3. Publicity Axis

Question: Where may this appear?

States:

- PRIVATE_DRAFT
- CANDIDATE
- INTERNAL_REPO
- PUBLIC_STATEMENT
- PUBLIC_WEBSITE
- BADGE_DISPLAY

Rule: public badge display requires a separate promotion receipt.

### 4. Access Axis

Question: What systems or lanes can this enter?

States:

- NO_ACCESS
- REVIEW_ACCESS
- INDEXER_ACCESS
- CANDIDATE_BADGE_ACCESS
- SERVICE_CANDIDATE_ACCESS
- LIVE_ACCESS

Rule: access means workflow permission, not institutional authority.

### 5. Jurisdiction / Vernacular Axis

Question: What local/global meaning does this claim carry?

States:

- LOCAL_PHRASE
- GLOBAL_MEME
- TECHNICAL_CLAIM
- CIVIC_CLAIM
- AGENCY_ADJACENT_LANGUAGE
- FIREWALL_SENSITIVE

Rule: vernacular becomes Wisdom only when phrase, context, claim, and evidence are separated.
