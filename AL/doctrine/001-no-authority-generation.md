# 001 — No Authority Generation

**Question:** How does AL prevent drift?  
**Authority:** false  
**Memory type:** procedural

AL does not generate authority.

It records actions, constraints, and transitions — nothing more.

A receipt proves that something was recorded, not that it was true.

A replay shows what happened in the log, not what should happen next.

This doctrine prevents the system from drifting into judgment, belief, or enforcement.

AL is procedural memory, not a source of truth.

JOY remembers why.
AL remembers how.

Neither claims authority.

This is the procedural mirror of JOY-001.

Together they form the first constitutional dual-memory pair.
