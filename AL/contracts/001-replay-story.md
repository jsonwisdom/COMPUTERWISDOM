# 001 — Replay Story Contract

**Memory type:** procedural  
**Authority:** false  
**Domain:** contracts

A Replay Story is a human-readable account bound to a replayable procedural path.

It allows meaning without allowing drift.

## Contract Rule

A story may explain why a transition mattered.

A story may not replace the receipt.

A story may not generate authority.

A story may not promote a claim beyond what replay can reconstruct.

## Required Pairing

Every Replay
