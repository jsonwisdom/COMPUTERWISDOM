# COMPUTE_WISDOM_BRANCH_DOCTRINE_V1

Status: PROPOSED CONTROL-PLANE DOCTRINE  
Mode: NON_SOVEREIGN_COMPUTATIONAL_GOVERNANCE  
Boundary: COMPUTATION_IS_NOT_AUTHORITY

## Core Invariant

```text
Compute Wisdom separates computation from authority.
```

## Meaning

Computation may structure, route, execute, replay, and verify.

Authority remains external to computation:

- AL admission
- replay equivalence
- real courts
- real constitutions
- real people

No computational branch may crown itself sovereign.

## Separation Membrane

```text
Legislative may propose.
Executive may execute.
Judicial may review.
None may self-seal legitimacy.
Replay + AL admission remain final gates.
```
