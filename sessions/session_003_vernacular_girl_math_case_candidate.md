## Purpose

Girl Math is added as a globally recognized vernacular case for the Confucius Indexer.

The goal is not to validate stereotypes.
The goal is to parse a viral phrase into claims, humor, behavioral economics, cultural context, risks, and receipts.

## Vernacular Description

Girl Math commonly refers to humorous or playful reasoning around purchases, value, discounts, cost-per-use, sunk costs, refunds, gift cards, and spending guilt.

Examples may include:

- if it was paid for earlier, it feels free now
- if cash was used, the card balance did not move
- if the item is used many times, the cost-per-use feels low
- if a discount was large, the saved money feels like new money

These are vernacular frames, not verified financial truths.

## Indexer Duties

For any Girl Math statement, the Confucius Indexer should:

1. Preserve the exact phrase.
2. Identify the local joke or cultural meaning.
3. Translate the logic into a claim map.
4. Separate humor from financial assertion.
5. Detect behavioral economics patterns.
6. Flag stereotype risk.
7. Tag claims as PUBLIC / HELD / PENDING / REJECTED.
8. Link evidence or mark missing evidence.
9. Produce a receipt-ready Wisdom Report.

## Claim Tags

PUBLIC:
- Girl Math is a viral internet vernacular pattern.
- It is often used humorously around spending rationalizations.

HELD:
- Any specific claim that a purchase is financially wise.
- Any claim that a group actually reasons this way universally.

PENDING:
- Source-specific claims about origin, reach, or trend size until cited.
- Behavioral economics mapping until evidence is linked.

REJECTED:
- Any essentialist claim that women or girls are inherently bad at math.
- Any claim that humor alone proves financial correctness.

## Safety Boundary

Girl Math must be indexed as culture and reasoning pattern, not as a gendered truth claim.

The indexer may analyze cognitive framing, mental accounting, cost-per-use, payment salience, discounts, and social media spread.

The indexer must not reinforce claims that girls or women are mathematically inferior.
