## Purpose

The Three Courts Multi-Vector layer brings Meme Court, Goblin Court, and Clown Court into the Game of Growth as classification and routing systems.

The courts do not create legal authority.
The courts do not declare fraud.
The courts do not prosecute.

They classify, route, pressure-test, translate, and generate receipt-backed public game outputs.

```text
Public data is byte-by-byte.
```

## Court Functions

### Meme Court

Meme Court translates receipt-backed observations into public-facing forms.

Allowed outputs:

- shareable cards
- slogans
- public explainers
- parody-safe art prompts
- civic game language

Blocked outputs:

- unsupported accusations
- harassment
- guilt declarations
- fraud declarations without lawful finding

### Goblin Court

Goblin Court protects receipt discipline.

Allowed outputs:

- needs_receipt
- admissible_observation
- unregistered_source_warning
- schema_drift_warning
- replay_required

Blocked outputs:

- accusation promotion
- source mutation without registry update
- hidden inference
- authority upgrade

### Clown Court

Clown Court detects absurdity, contradiction, and overreach.

Allowed outputs:

- authority_drift_flag
- contradiction_flag
- narrative_overreach_flag
- unsupported_claim_flag
- absurdity_badge

Blocked outputs:

- personal attack
- harassment
- legal conclusion
- enforcement direction

No invisible vector mutation is allowed.

## Reputation Rule

Reputation is based on receipt quality, not accusation intensity.

Allowed reputation signals:

- valid receipt submitted
- replay succeeded
- source registered
- correction accepted
- counter-receipt provided
- public explanation was accurate

Blocked reputation signals:

- calling something fraud without receipt
- targeting a person
- popularity-only claims
- harassment engagement
- unsupported narratives

## PII Membrane

Public game surfaces must not expose private personal information.

```json
{
  "public_data": "BYTE_BY_BYTE_PUBLIC_RECORDS",
  "pii": "RESTRICTED_TO_LAWFUL_OR_PRIVATE_COURT_HANDLING_SURFACES_ONLY",
  "public_display_of_pii": false,
  "doxxing": false,
  "private_targeting": false,
  "authority": false
}
```

PII handling rules:

1. Public records may be hashed, compared, and receipted.
2. PII must be minimized or redacted before public game display.
3. If PII is necessary for a lawful referral packet, it must be routed outside the public game layer.
4. Courts in the game may classify PII sensitivity, but they do not gain legal authority.
5. Public parody art must not target private citizens.

## Court Authority Clarification

The word court is a game metaphor and classification surface.

In-game courts can:

- classify
- flag
- route
- translate
- request receipts
- mark replay status

In-game courts cannot:

- adjudicate guilt
- issue legal findings
- prosecute
- compel action
- expose private data
- override lawful institutions
