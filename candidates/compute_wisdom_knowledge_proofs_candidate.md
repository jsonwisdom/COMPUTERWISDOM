# Compute Wisdom Knowledge Proofs

Status: Candidate only, not live.
Operator: jaywisdom.base.eth
Surface: /candidates/
Mutation rule: do not overwrite portal.html.
Promotion: requires hash, Jay approval, and promotion receipt.

## Purpose

Compute Wisdom Knowledge Proofs turn skill-building into reviewable evidence.

A learner does not claim mastery. A learner submits a proof object.

Each proof should record:

1. What was learned
2. What source or task was used
3. What output was produced
4. What verification step was performed
5. What receipt anchors the result

## Tags

HELD means not yet proven.
PENDING means submitted for review.
PUBLIC means visible proof with sufficient receipt.
REJECTED means failed verification.
PROMOTED means Jay-approved candidate proof.

## Badge Rule

No badge becomes public unless linked to a Knowledge Proof.

Badge without proof is decoration. Badge with proof is replay surface.

## Canon

Compute Wisdom turns learning into receipts. Skill is not asserted. Skill is proven.
