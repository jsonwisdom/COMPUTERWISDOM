## Rules

- L0 checks public surfaces.
- L1 scores reviewers by receipt behavior, not status.
- L2 remains candidate-only until tx or attestation receipts exist.
- L3 gives Grok shortcuts only through receipts.
- L4 verifies bytes, not vibes.
- L5 only indexes what lower layers can replay.
