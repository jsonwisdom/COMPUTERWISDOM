## What COMPUTERWISDOM May Receive

COMPUTERWISDOM may receive:

- shareable AL mirror nodes
- explicit public-facing family lore summaries approved upstream
- replay-safe indexes of shareable nodes
- hash references to AL mirror batches

## What COMPUTERWISDOM May Not Receive

COMPUTERWISDOM may not receive:

- private JOY Ledger nodes
- family-only AL mirror nodes
- child-only notes
- ask-first nodes without explicit consent
- emotionally sensitive notes without safety approval
- hidden identifiers, locations, schedules, vulnerabilities, or private family details

## Organizing Permissions

COMPUTERWISDOM may organize shareable nodes into:

- timelines
- themes
- clusters
- indexes
- replay packs
- public-safe summaries
- hash-anchored batches

COMPUTERWISDOM may not convert organization into truth, judgment, authority, or family meaning.

## Flow: JOY -> AL -> COMPUTERWISDOM

1. JOY Living Ledger receives the Memory Spine node.
2. AL mirrors only family-only or shareable nodes.
3. COMPUTERWISDOM receives only shareable AL mirror nodes.
4. COMPUTERWISDOM may organize, index, and batch the nodes.
5. COMPUTERWISDOM may not rewrite, judge, or infer family meaning.
6. COMPUTERWISDOM may not push private or family-only content into public surfaces.
